About Our App

Empowering you with tools for optimizing your stock portfolio using advanced financial models.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced financial tools and empower individuals to better understand the complexities of portfolio optimization, all in an easy-to-use and accessible platform.

What We Do

Our app provides a comprehensive platform for investors to download and analyze stock data, and construct optimal portfolios using the Harry Markowitz Efficient Frontier model. With a focus on educational and testing purposes, our tool offers insights into portfolio optimization, helping you understand the principles of modern portfolio theory.

Step 1: Download Stock Data

Using free data from yfinance, our app allows you to download historical stock prices for your chosen companies. Simply enter the stock symbols, and the app will retrieve the relevant data for analysis.

Step 2: Calculate Optimal Portfolio

Our app utilizes the Harry Markowitz Efficient Frontier model to help you calculate the optimal portfolio. By analyzing the risk-return trade-offs, the app suggests the best combination of stocks to maximize returns for a given level of risk.

Create multiple lists of favorite stocks, each list containing between 5 and 50 stocks. Simulate portfolios using our Simulation function to create optimal portfolios.

Step 3: Track Your Portfolio

Track your portfolio's performance over time, making it easier to monitor your investments and make informed decisions.

Important Disclaimer

This app is intended for educational and testing purposes only. It does not provide investment advice and should not be used as a sole source for making investment decisions. The developers and creators of this app are not responsible for any financial losses incurred as a result of using the app.

Always consult with a qualified financial advisor before making any investment decisions.
