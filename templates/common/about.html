About Our App

Empowering you with tools for optimizing your stock portfolio using advanced financial models.

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to advanced financial tools and empower individuals to better understand the complexities of portfolio optimization, all in an easy-to-use and accessible platform.

What We Do

Our platform provides a comprehensive tool for investors and finance enthusiasts to access and analyze stock data using the free Yahoo Finance API. Users can create unlimited favorite stock lists, organizing their chosen stocks into customized collections. At the core of the application is the simulation feature, capable of running up to 20,000 simulations on a range of date intervals, starting from January 1st, 2010, to the present. Utilizing the Harry Markowitz Efficient Frontier model, the simulation offers a practical introduction to modern portfolio theory, helping users explore the principles of diversification and optimal portfolio construction.

Step 1: Retrieve Stock Data

Leverage our integration with the free Yahoo Finance API to access historical stock data. Simply enter the ticker symbols of your preferred companies, and the app will download comprehensive price data for analysis, dating back to January 1st, 2010.

Step 2: Run Simulations for Optimal Portfolios

Utilize the powerful Harry Markowitz Efficient Frontier model to explore the risk-return balance of your chosen stocks. Create lists of favorite stocks with up to 50 tickers each, and use our advanced simulation feature to run up to 20,000 scenarios, helping you determine the most efficient portfolio based on historical data and risk preferences.

Step 3: Monitor and Analyze Your Portfolio

Keep track of your portfolio's performance over time with detailed analytics and historical value tracking. Monitor daily returns and analyze trends, enabling you to make informed, data-driven decisions and optimize your investment strategy as market conditions evolve.
